Beaver

Beavers have webbed hind feet with 5 toes (4.5-7"). Hind prints often wipe out front prints, and their tail may erase all tracks.

Porcupine

Porcupines move slowly and have big heel pads and long toes with claws. Prints (1-2") show mostly pads with rough surfaces for climbing.

Muskrat

Muskrat tracks are hand-like like raccoons but smaller (2-3"). Five long toes on hind feet, four on front, found near marshes and ponds.
