Small Mammals

  • Small to medium paw prints (under 2 inches)
  • Often show four or five toes
  • Tracks frequently appear in groups or patterns

Raccoon

Raccoons have five toes resembling a human hand. Front print is smaller (1-3") with C-shaped heel pad, rear print has longer heel pad (1.5-4").

Opossum tracks resemble raccoons but have opposable thumbs on hind feet - the only North American mammal with this feature.

Skunk
