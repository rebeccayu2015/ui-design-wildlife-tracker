Canines

  • Oval shape with four toes and a heel pad that is concave at the bottom
  • Four toes that point forward with two front toes lined up
  • Claws visible and point forward
  • Fore prints significantly larger than hind print
  • Can draw an "X" through the print

Wolf

The wolf print is much like a domestic dog but larger, measuring between 4 and 5" long. Wolves tend to direct register their prints, leaving a straighter line.
